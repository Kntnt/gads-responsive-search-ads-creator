## Distribution Analysis

- **Keyword match (5):** Headline 1–5
- **Benefit/advantage (3):** Headline 6–8
- **Credibility (2):** Headline 9–10
- **Differentiation (2):** Headline 11–12
- **CTA (3):** Headline 13–15

Note how:

- Keyword headlines vary the form: "camera surveillance", "camera monitoring", "surveillance camera" – never the exact same term twice.
- Benefit headlines are concrete ("Reduces Property Damage") rather than abstract ("High Security").
- CTA headlines (pos 3) vary: one general, one with incentive, one audience-targeted.
- Descriptions develop the message – they don't repeat headlines but complement them.
- All descriptions are comfortably under the 90-character limit while packing in substantial information.
